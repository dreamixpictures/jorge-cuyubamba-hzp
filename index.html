Huazhong Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd (HPC), established in 1995, is the representative of leading API manufacturers in China. Our product portfolio includes APIs for human and veterinary use, Vitamins, food, feed and nutrition products.

We provide APIs from the most qualified and well-known Chinese manufacturers with current GMP and COS documents and FDA or DMF certificates. Furthermore, HPC’s main products include Vitamins and Vitamin premixes with pharma, food, feed and natural grade as well as food and nutrition products. All materials meet CGMP-Standards and can be provided with KOSHER and HALAL certifications.

We act as the exclusive distributor for many leading Chinese API manufacturers. We support our manufacturers in the whole process of documentation and development to facilitate generic drug market entry prior to expiration of the patent. Moreover, we assist our foreign clients to find reliable manufacturers in China
