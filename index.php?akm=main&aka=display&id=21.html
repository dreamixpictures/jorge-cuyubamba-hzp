      History




      HPC 简介

      1995

      華中药业有限公司在香港成立。同年推出Vitamins B1,B2,B5,B6,B12。
      Huazhong Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd, founded in Hongkong, mainly dealing with business of Vitamins, B1, B2, B5, B6 & B12 etc

      1997

      深圳華中药业有限公司成立。同年推出阿莫西林，扑热息痛，叶酸，奈普生，格列齐特，Vitamin C 等人用药产品。
      Huazhong Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd. Shenzhen founded, more Human used APIs business developed, such as, Amoxicilline, Paracetamol, Folic acid, Naproxen, Gliclazide, Vitamin C etc.

      2000

      HPC 成立上海公司。同年推出强力霉素，硫酸辛霉素，林可霉素 等兽药产品。
      Huazhong Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd. Shanghai founded, veterinary APIs business involved, Doxycycline Hyclate, Neomycin Sulfate, Lincomycin HCl.

      2005

      HPC 法规部门成立。同年推出生物素，三氯蔗糖，氨糖，软骨素，硫辛酸，Vitamin E。
      HPC regulatory department established, more vitamin & nutrition items added to company profiles, such as Biotin, Sucralose, Glucosamine, chondroitin, lipoic acid, and Vitamin E.

      2008

      HPC 建立亚洲市场分销基地。创立TOP 100产品分销模式。
      Distribution system of Top 100 items for Asian market built

      2010

      HPC 成立资本运营部。同年入股安徽九方制药有限公司。九方制药拥有四大天然植物药，专利原料药和制剂产品。
      Capital management department established, joined Anhui Joyfar Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd, with four herbal extracts, patented raw materials & final products.

      2011

      九方资本有限公司成立。同年上海九方投资有限公司创立。
      Joyfar Capital Limited Company founded, and Shanghai Joyfar investment Co., Ltd. founded at the same year.

      2013

      HPC 上海公司更名为上海九華进出口有限公司。同年成立生命科学部门，推出获得FDA/COS 认证的首仿药品。
      Life sciences department founded, engaged in more FDA/COS certified generic APIs business.

      2015

      HPC 20周年CPHI 庆祝会圆满成功。来自世界各地客户分享HPC 参股的法国波尔多美酒。
      20 Anniversary CPHI ceremony successfully held, sharing the wine with customers and friends of all over the worlds, which is from HPC joint Bordeaux chateau.

      2017

      HPC 成立资产并购和合同订制海外事业部。
      Asset acquisition department and OEM department founded.
