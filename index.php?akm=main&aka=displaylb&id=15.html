      Traditional Chinese Medicine




      Joyfar Pharmaceuticals:

      - HPC invests in Joyfar Pharmaceuticals and collaborates with the factory on the development and production of Traditional Chinese Medicine.

      - Joyfar Pharmaceuticals was established in 1993 in Bozhou, Anhui Province/China, which is regarded as being the ‘Chinese Herbal Medicine City’

      - The company is focused in the development and production of Traditional Chinese Medicine from herbals.

      - The company covers an area of 20,000㎡.

      - At present, it has 300 employees, over 70% with related technical qualification.

      - A workshop with 5000t/annual production capability is available for contract manufacturing.

      - The workshops have both GAP and GMP certifications and meet international requirements.

      - The company has 10 product lines including tablets, capsules, granules, oral solutions, liquor and tea agents.

      - Finished Products: Medicine for cardiovascular diseases, Medicine for digestion systems, Anti-flu medicine, Nutrition

      - For more information please visit www.jfpharm.com
